What I do

Throughout my journey as a UX/UI Designer, I have had the opportunity to work on a number of different projects. My experiences have allowed me to gather an arsenal of skills that make me not only an effective worker, but a versatile designer.

Coding

One of my most treasured skills. I have held a role in my project groups as the technical savant. I consider myself proficient in HTML/CSS, with experience using JS, Java, Python and C++. However, this is only the beginning of this list. I hope future work/projects will make this list grow!

Project Management

I have worked on multiple projects where I have served as either a Project Lead or a Co-Lead. My experience is mainly in utilizing Lean UX to drive my design process. However, I also have knowledge of Agile Development methods

Collaboration

I am a strong believer that collaborative work creates the finest solutions. Personally, I love bouncing ideas off peers and getting feedback on my work. During my time at Purdue, I have worked in many collaborative teams for classes/projects. Thus, collaboration and small group communication are nothing new to me.

Wireframing and Prototyping

With a strong passion for creating modern, responsive UI's, I have used many tools to aid in visualizing in my designs. I am most proficient with Sketch and Figma. I also have extensive experience using the Adobe Creative Suite in order to create final deliverables.
